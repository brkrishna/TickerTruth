# Pricing

## Early Bundle Tiers

### Explorer
**Free or INR 5,000/month**
- Limited sample tables (20 symbols)
- Last 2 years of symbol lineage
- Public corporate action events only
- Delayed updates (2–4 weeks behind)
- **Use case:** Trial and evaluation

### Starter
**INR 15,000–35,000/month**
- Full NSE symbol lineage history
- 3+ years of corporate action events
- Monthly versioned releases
- Email support
- **Use case:** Small teams, backtesting, research

### Professional
**INR 40,000–1,00,000/month**
- Full history including private equity delisting data
- Adjustment factors and QA confidence scores
- Custom date ranges and exports
- Priority email support
- **Use case:** Production quant systems, broker platforms

### Enterprise
**INR 1.5 lakh+/month**
- Dedicated data refresh schedule
- BSE add-ons and custom mappings
- Bespoke exports and integrations
- Direct support channels
- **Use case:** Large institutional players, platforms

## Why This Pricing Works

Priced as a trust layer, not raw data volume. We save:
- Engineering time on data cleanup
- Months of reconciliation work
- Risk of broken analytics pipelines
